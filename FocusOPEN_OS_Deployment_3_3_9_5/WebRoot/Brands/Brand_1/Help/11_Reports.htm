Asset Statistics

Available to: Upload Users & All Admin
[top ^ | close]

Details stats for an individual asset. These stats can be viewed by the Upload User who uploaded the asset and Super Administrators. Asset Stats are accessible via the Stats link, available on the Asset Details pop-up window beneath the asset preview.

Asset stats include: asset preview, key metadata, asset activity (downloads, orders, views, search hits, added to cart and added to lightbox) displayed in both numeric and graphic formats for that asset, top ten search terms for that asset, last ten users to download that asset and asset audit trail (available in CSV format).

Registration & Login Reports

Available to: Brand & Super Admin
[top ^ | close]

The registration and login reports show login and registration figures across companies, brands and timeframes. Statistics like how many new users registered within a certain timeframe can be generated. The report summaries for both logins and registrations include: the number of registrations or logins at the start of the selected timeframe, the number of registrations or logins at the end of the selected timeframe and the actual and percentage rise for that timeframe.

Figures can be accrued (i.e. the previous month's total can be added to the next month's total to show a graph on which the totals for each month rise across timeframe) or can be unaccrued (to display a graph on which the totals for each month may rise and fall across the timeframe).

Audit Trial

Available to: Super Admin Only
[top ^ | close]

The audit trail details system activity for users or IP addresses, across specific date ranges and system activities (for example, logging out), for the auditing purposes. For example, the activities of a specific user on the system in a specific day or hour, can be viewed.

Audit trail reports can be viewed in the browser or can be downloaded in .CSV format.
