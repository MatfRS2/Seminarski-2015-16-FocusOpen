Logging In

[top ^ | close]

Login

To access FocusOPEN Digital Asset Manager, you must first log into the system:

  1. Enter your email address into the "Email" field
  2. Enter you password into the "Password" field
  3. Press the Login button to log into the system
    Note: Keep your account secure. Do not share your password with anyone else.

If your login is successful, you will be presented with FocusOPEN Digital Asset Manager Homepage. From here you can navigate all the main features of the system, including searching or browsing for digital assets.

If your login is unsuccessful, please check your details and try again. If you are still unable to log in, contact your system administrator for further information.

Tip: If you use FocusOPEN Digital Asset Manager regularly from the same computer, select the "Remember my email next time" checkbox to auto-fill the Email field with your email address each time you log in.

Forgot Password?

[top ^ | close]

Password Reminders

If you have forgotten your password, the system can send a new password to your registered email address. To send a new password to your inbox:

  1. Enter your email address into the "Email" field
  2. Press the "Forgot Password?" button to send a new password to your inbox
  3. Log into the system with your new password and email address, as above.

Logging Out

[top ^ | close]

Logout

You should log out of FocusOPEN Digital Asset Manager after each session. To log out, click the "Logout" link which is available on all system interfaces on the top right-hand corner of the main navigation. On successful logout, you will be returned to the Login screen. Note: If your account is idle for more than 4 hours, you will be automatically logged out of the system.

Registration

[top ^ | close]

How To Register

To have an account with FocusOPEN Digital Asset Manager, you must be a registered system user. To access the registration screen, click on the "Register Here" link on the Login screen or the "Register" link which is available on the bottom right-hand corner of the footer navigation.

To register with the system:

  1. Complete all relevant fields on the Registration Form
    Note: Fields marked '*' are required


  2. Press the "Register" button to submit your Registration
    Note: Pressing the "Cancel" button will quit the Registration process and any details will not be submitted.


    3. Internal Users: Employees

  3. If the registration application is made by an employee with a company email address, then the system will automatically dispatch login details in a confirmation email to the applicant's registered inbox. Applicants must click on the link contained in the email to log into the system and activate their account.


    4. Approved External Users: Agencies, Freelancers And Consultants

  4. If the registration application is made by an individual who isn't an employee but whose organisation has been approved for access, their application will be sent to an Administrator for approval. On approval of their application, a confirmation will be dispatched to the applicant's registered inbox. Applicants must click on the link contained in the email to log into the system and activate their account.
    5. Users who are not approved for system access by an Administrator will be informed via email.

Note: The Registration feature can be enabled or disabled by system Administrators. See the Administrators Guide for further information.

Edit Profile

[top ^ | close]
To edit your profile, click on the "Edit Profile" link on the My Account screen. The Edit Profile screen will be displayed:
  1. Complete all relevant fields on the Edit Profile Form
    Note: Fields marked '*' are required
  2. Press the "Save Changes" button to save the changes to your profile.

    3. Note: Pressing the "Cancel" button will quit the Edit process and any changes will not be saved.
