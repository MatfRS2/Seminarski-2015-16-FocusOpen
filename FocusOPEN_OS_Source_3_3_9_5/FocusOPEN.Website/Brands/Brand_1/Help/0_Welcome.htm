Gaining Access

[top ^ | close]

To gain access to FocusOPEN Digital Asset Manager, you must first register with the system or request access from your systems administrator. If no registration is provided. Please note: FocusOPEN Digital Asset Manager is not available to the general public. Attempting unauthorised access to this facility is forbidden and will result in action being taken against you. On registration your IP address will be logged and your request will be considered by a member of staff before access is granted.
